Winners on and off the Field

Through the course of history, there are three men who have won the World Cup tittle as both Managers and Players. These men are the following:

Mario Zagallo

Zagallo won the World Cup as a player in 1958 and 1962 and years later won as a manager in 1970 bringing him to a total of three world cup trophies.

Franz Beckenbauer

Beckenbauer won the World Cup as a player in 1974 and years later won as a manager in 1990 bringing him to a total of two world cup trophies.

Didier Deschamps

Deschamps won the World Cup as a player in 1998 and years later won as a manager in 2018 bringing him to a total of two world cup trophies.
