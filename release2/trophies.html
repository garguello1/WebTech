World Cup Trophies

The tournament consists of 32 nations competing for one trophy yet, there are several other awards handed out to individual players according to their performances throughout the cup.

Golden Ball

This award is given to the player with the best overall performance throughout the tournament.

The Golden Boot

This award is given to the player or players with the most goals throughout the tournament.

The Golden Glove

This award is given to the best performing goalkeeper of the tournament.

All-Star Team

Although this is not an award, the top players of the tournament compete for a spot in the All-Star team of the World Cup in order to increase their fanbases and increase their likelyhood of being singed by a top performing club the following season.
